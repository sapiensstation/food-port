04 Key Success Factors
⭐
Reviews = Revenue
5-star Google reviews are your #1 growth engine. Ask every happy customer immediately after the job.
🏢
Chase Fleets
One landscaping company with 6 trucks can pay $1,500–3,000/month in recurring maintenance. Target them early.
🧾
Know Your Numbers
Track every job's labor hours and parts cost. Most new operators underprice by 20–30%.
📍
Stay in Your Zone
Limit travel radius to 15–20 miles when starting. Drive time is unpaid time.
🛠️
Stick to Core Services
Oil changes, brakes, batteries, starters, alternators, and diagnostics. Decline jobs needing a lift or alignment rack.
📦
Parts Markup = Profit
Mark parts up 30–50% above cost. This is standard practice and often equals or exceeds your labor profit.
